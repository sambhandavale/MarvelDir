The Dark World (2013)


Thor: The Dark World (2013) sees Thor battling an ancient enemy, Malekith, who seeks to plunge the universe into darkness using a powerful substance called the Aether. Thor teams up with Loki to stop this threat, putting aside their differences. The film delves into Asgardian lore, explores the relationship between the brothers, and highlights the sacrifice required to protect the realms. It also introduces the concept of the Convergence.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014) follows the misfit group of space outlaws, including Star-Lord, Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot, as they come together to stop the villain Ronan the Accuser from using a powerful cosmic artifact. Avengers

Age of Ultron 2015


Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015) sees the Avengers reassembling to combat Ultron, a sentient AI created by Tony Stark and Bruce Banner, who seeks to bring about human extinction. Alongside new allies, the team faces internal conflicts and confronts their own vulnerabilities. Ant-man

2015


Ant-Man (2015) follows Scott Lang, a skilled thief, who becomes the new Ant-Man under the mentorship of Dr. Hank Pym. Using a suit that grants him the ability to shrink in size while increasing in strength, Lang must pull off a heist to prevent Pym's technology from falling into the wrong hands. The film combines humor, clever action sequences, and a unique shrinking perspective, offering a refreshing take on the superhero genre within the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
