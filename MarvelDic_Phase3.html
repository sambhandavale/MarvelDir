Captain America Civil War

2016


In Captain America: Civil War, the Avengers clash over government oversight, led by Captain America and Iron Man. The conflict deepens when Bucky Barnes is accused of a terrorist act, dividing the team further. The film explores themes of accountability, friendship, and loyalty amidst intense action sequences. Ultimately, the Avengers face internal and external challenges that reshape their relationships and the future of superhero regulation.

Doctor Strange

2016


In Doctor Strange, brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon Stephen Strange seeks healing after a devastating car accident, leading him to mystical powers. Trained by the Ancient One, he learns sorcery and defends Earth from dark forces, including Kaecilius, who seeks eternal life. Through mind-bending visuals and spiritual growth, Strange embraces his role as a guardian of the mystical realms. The film explores themes of ego, redemption, and the nature of reality, introducing audiences to Marvel's magical universe and setting the stage for Strange's pivotal role in the Avengers.


Director

Scott Derrickson

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 2

2017


In Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 , Peter Quill and the Guardians navigate familial revelations while evading threats. They encounter Ego, Peter's celestial father, who harbors sinister intentions. The team grapples with personal conflicts, including Gamora's relationship with her sister Nebula and Rocket's insecurities. Amidst humor and dazzling visuals, the Guardians unite to thwart Ego's plan to dominate the universe, reaffirming their bond as a dysfunctional yet loving family.


Director

James Gunn

Thor

Thor 1 tells the story of the arrogant Asgardian prince, Thor, who is banished to Earth by his father Odin. Stripped of his powers, Thor learns humility and the true meaning of being a hero. He must prove himself worthy to reclaim his hammer, Mjolnir, and protect Earth from his vengeful brother, Loki.

The First Avenger 2011


Captain America: The First Avenger follows Steve Rogers, a scrawny young man who volunteers for an experimental super-soldier program during World War II.
